About Me

Hello! My name is Katie (well, actually it's Katarina) and I live in Durham, North Carolina. I grew up on a farm in northern Maryland (only half a mile from Pennsylvania!), spent nine years living in Portland, Oregon, and returned to the east coast in early 2014 to see what the South had to offer.

I have driven across the country five times, have two Oregon-born cats, and like to spend my free time eating spicy foods and fixing up my house -- some photos can be found under the #cutehouseiscute tag on Instagram. Some of my favorite things to do in Durham are to go hiking on the Eno and explore new breweries in the area!
